Scheffler Concentrator

Title: Scheffler Concentrator

Duration: October 2010-May 2011
Research and Development Project (AKSON'S Solar)
Description:

A technology transfer MoU was signed with Mr. Wolfgang Scheffler-an innovator himself, for manufacturing Scheffler Concentrator as an industrial product.

This technology was incorporated for two different end usages

  • Mass cooking: A concentrator was directly used as a heat source and the receiver depicted the cooking spot in the system. Such a system can be used in hostels and community kitchens.

  • Process Steam Generation: A circular dish receiver was used to generate steam at ambient pressure to be used as process steam for various end usages.

  • A target niche market of community kitchens in India.

  • Process steam generation

  • A commercial product for the company
